At Chic Chain (https://mychicchain.com) ("we," "our," "us"), we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

  • Contact Information: Name, surname, email address, phone number, mailing address, and any other details you provide when contacting us or registering on our site.

1.2 Information We Automatically Collect

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Details about your interactions with our website, including pages visited, time spent on the site, and clickstream data.

We may receive information about you from third parties, such as social media platforms or partners, when you interact with our content or services on their platforms.

  • To Provide Our Services: To process orders, manage accounts, and deliver products or services you request.
  • To Improve Our Website: To analyze usage trends and enhance the functionality and user experience of our website.
  • To Communicate With You: To respond to inquiries, send account updates, and provide marketing materials (with your consent).
  • To Process Payments: To handle transactions securely and verify payment details.
  • For Marketing and Advertising: To personalize and display relevant ads and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
  • For Legal and Compliance Purposes: To comply with laws and protect rights, privacy, safety, or property.

3. Legal Basis for Processing Your Information

  • Consent: When you provide explicit consent for specific purposes.
  • Performance of a Contract: To deliver requested products or services.
