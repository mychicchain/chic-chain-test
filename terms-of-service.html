1. About Chic Chain

1.1 Overview

Chic Chain operates the online fashion marketplace on the domain mychicchain.com, on the Telegram channel @mychicchain, and via Telegram bot @mychicchain_bot (collectively, "Chic Chain Platform" or "Chic Chain") and connects both Renters with Lenders and Buyers with Sellers on the website and through the associated Telegram channel.

1.2 Our Mission

Chic Chain aims to revolutionize ownership by offering convenient solutions for a sustainable wardrobe. This includes Renting, Lending, and Buying or Selling pre-owned clothing and accessories through our Website and Telegram channel.

1.3 Role of Chic Chain

The Chic Chain Platform facilitates transactions between Members, allowing them to:

  • Renter: Rent Product(s) from a Lender via the Chic Chain Platform.
  • Lender: Make available Product(s) to Rent and Lend Products to Renters via the Chic Chain Platform.
  • Seller: Make available Products for Sale to Buyers on the Chic Chain Platform.
  • Buyer: Purchase Products on the Chic Chain Platform from a Seller.

Chic Chain acts as an intermediary to facilitate these transactions but is not a party to any contract between users for renting, lending, selling, or buying goods.
